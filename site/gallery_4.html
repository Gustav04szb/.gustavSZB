Future School Concept Visualization

During my time working towards my advanced technical diploma (Fachabitur), we had a themed project week focused on interdisciplinary learning. Our task was to develop a vision of what schools might look like in the future and create a concept for a presentation. I created these renders to visualize one of the proposed ideas.

Household Robot – English Class Project

For an English class presentation, I created a concept for a household robot designed to assist with everyday tasks. To make the idea more convincing, I found a fitting 3D model and rendered it in various poses and designs to showcase its functionality and visual appeal.
